Chicken Milanese

Description

Make our breaded chicken Milanese - the cheese is optional, but it takes the coating to the next level. You can also make it with turkey, pork or aubergine

Ingredients

  • Eggs : Keep the chicken moist and give the breading something to adhere to.
  • Seasonings: The breading is simply seasoned with salt and pepper.
  • Bread crumbs: Use store-bought or find recipe creator Sherbear1's homemade bread crumb recipe below.
  • Chicken: Two thinly sliced, skinless, boneless chicken breast halves will make four servings.
  • Oil: Use vegetable oil for frying the chicken Milanese.
  • Lemon : Serve the chicken Milanese with lemon wedges for a bright pop of color and flavor.

How to Make Chicken Milanese

  1. "You can buy chicken cutlets, but I find it's easier (and a bit of a better value) to buy boneless skinless chicken breasts and cut them in half yourself," -Nicole.
  2. To ensure even cooking, pound the cutlets with a meat mallet before you add the breading. This will also give you a larger surface, which means it will get extra crispy.
  3. Make sure to have a wire rack handy for draining so the bottoms do not get soggy while you work on the other cutlets.
